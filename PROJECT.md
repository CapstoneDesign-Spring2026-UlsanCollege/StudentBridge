# StudentBridge

## Project Overview

StudentBridge is a web platform designed to help international students find part-time job opportunities during their studies. Many foreign students struggle to find suitable jobs due to language barriers, lack of local work experience, and limited information about available opportunities.

StudentBridge aims to bridge this gap by connecting international students with employers who are willing to hire student workers.

## Problem Statement

International students often face difficulties finding part-time jobs because:

* Language barriers make communication difficult.
* Many employers prefer candidates with local work experience.
* Students have limited knowledge of available job opportunities.

These challenges make it harder for students to support themselves financially while studying abroad.

## Proposed Solution

StudentBridge provides a centralized platform where international students can easily search and apply for part-time jobs. Employers can also post job openings specifically targeted toward students.

The platform will simplify the job search process and improve accessibility for international students.

## Target Users

* International students looking for part-time jobs
* Employers seeking student workers
* University communities

## Key Features

### Student Features

* User registration and login
* Create student profile
* Upload resume
* Search part-time job listings
* Apply for jobs
* Save favorite job postings

### Employer Features

* Employer registration and login
* Post job opportunities
* Manage job listings
* View student applications

## Expected Benefits

* Helps international students find part-time work more easily
* Connects employers with motivated student workers
* Provides a centralized job-search platform for students
